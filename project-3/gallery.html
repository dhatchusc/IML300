Instead of a gallery of biracial babies that you might easily find on the internet, this is a gallery of biracial adults. The multiracial experience encapsulates a lot of people with diverse life experiences and identities.

The only way to view an individual's baby picture is to click, where you will see their name and often a quote about their experiences. This way multiracial people are no longer objects to be viewed on the internet but people with names and life experiences.
